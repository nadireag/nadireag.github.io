Air Quality

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is used for reporting daily air quality. It tells you how clean or polluted your air is. This project makes analysis for North Carolina and some other US cities. What are the main polutants? Is there any relationship between weather and air quality - such as high temperature and air pressure. The data is retrieved using the IQAir Visual API.
